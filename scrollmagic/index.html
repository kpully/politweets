Aggregating congressional tweets, one 3AM rant at a time


Congressional tweets have increased by over 450% since 2012, however, there is little oversight into what is being said or how policy is being talked about. Politweets sets out with two goals (1) Pull together tweets in an open, searchable format and (2) Explore the types of messages that congresspeople are sharing with the world. Through analysis of healthcare, immigration, and other information we have learned a few things:

1. Politicians don't always use the *correct* name of legislation
2. Tweets often favor policies they oppose.

Use of the term ObamaCare versus ACA

Since 2009 we found over 8,000 tweets mentioning ACA or ObamaCare. Given the confusion over whether or not these are the same thing, we setout to understand how the different parties were talking about the two policies. Not surprisingly, we overwhelmingly found that Democrats tweet about "ACA" or "Affordable Care Act", while Republicans tweet about ObamaCare.
